# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming and respectful experience for everyone.

---

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful
- Be professional
- Accept constructive feedback
- Help newcomers
- Focus on collaboration

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment
- Personal attacks
- Offensive language
- Spam
- Discrimination
- Trolling

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, or pull requests that violate this Code of Conduct.

Repeated violations may result in a ban from contributing.

---

Thank you for helping build a positive community around TestPilot AI.
